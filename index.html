The Skater

Stefan
Janoski

Stefan Janoski is widely regarded as one of the most influential skateboarders of his generation, known for a style that blends technical skill with creativity and flow. Rather than following trends, he helped define a more expressive, refined approach to skateboarding that has inspired generations. His influence extends beyond the board into art and design, making him a cultural figure whose impact continues to shape the identity of skateboarding.

The Pipeline

The Education
& Membership
Pipeline

This project is designed as a cultural-to-education pipeline.

A limited run of skate decks, designed by Stefan Janoski, serves as the entry point. Each deck includes an integration zine and helps fund additional copies distributed for free through skate shops.

The zine becomes the bridge. Inside, a QR code unlocks exclusive playlists curated by Stefan, requiring email access.

That moment converts cultural engagement into a direct connection, allowing us to introduce users to deeper educational resources and MAPS membership.

A cultural moment becomes a pathway — from skate shop floor to psychedelic education.
